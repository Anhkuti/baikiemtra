The Walk

The walk Monterosso to Riomanggiore will take you approximately two hours, give or take an hour depending on the weather conditions and your physical shape

What ?
Cinque Terre comprises five villages: Monterosso, Vernazza, Corniglia, Manarola, And Riomaggiore.
Where ?
on the northwest cost of the Italian Riviera, north of the city La Spezia.
Price ?
The Walk is free!
